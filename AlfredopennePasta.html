Penne Alfredo is a super quick and easy pasta recipe perfect any night of the week! With a simple creamy,
cheese sauce and chewy al dente pasta,it works perfectly on its own or as a side to meat dishes.
Penne alfredo pasta is a dish that the whole family will love!

Recipe


At first keep all your ingredients ready ,take a vessel and boil 4cups of water for 2 cups of pasta ,Now add pasta in boiling water with a pinch of salt in it and few drops of oil ,boil it for 5mins and strain the water.
And in another vessel add butter .Once the butter has melted, add the garlic and salt and cook for 1 minute, stirring as it cooks then stir fry the vegetables of ur choice such as corn,broccoli,bell peppers,
olives,mushrooms or chicken ,shrimp if you are a non vegetarian person.Then add little flour to your butter and pour some milk in the stirred veggies,add cheese of ur preferred quantity.
Now that your pasta sauce it ready.Add boiled pasta to your sauce and mix it well ,cook the pasta in sauce for 5mins .
now your pasta is ready ,you can garnish it with finely chopped parsley, black pepper and additional parmesan cheese.
